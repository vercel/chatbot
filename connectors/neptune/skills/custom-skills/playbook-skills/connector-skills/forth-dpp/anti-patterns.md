# Forth DPP Anti-Patterns — BANNED

## CRITICAL
- **Filing without client authorization**: Legal violation.
- **Same evidence for multiple rounds**: Bureau rejects as "previously investigated".
- **Missing 30-day response window**: Dispute dismissed.
- **Disputing accurate information**: FCRA violation.

## HIGH
- **"Scattershot" filing (all items at once)**: Frivolous classification.
- **Emotional language in letters**: Weakens legal standing.
- **Skipping furnisher notification**: Incomplete process.

## MEDIUM
- **Wrong reason code**: Bureau rejects incorrectly coded disputes.
- **No deadline tracking**: 30-day clock is hard — miss it, dispute dismissed.
