# GHL Anti-Patterns — BANNED

## CRITICAL
- **Contacting without DNC check**: Legal violation, TCPA risk.
- **Missing consent for SMS**: TCPA requires explicit opt-in.
- **Sending marketing to support contacts**: Wrong context, annoyance.

## HIGH
- **Duplicate contacts**: Fragmenting interaction history.
- **Wrong pipeline stage assignment**: Messes up reporting.
- **Missing lead source**: Can't track attribution.
